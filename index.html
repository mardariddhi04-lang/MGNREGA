What is MGNREGA?

The Mahatma Gandhi National Rural Employment Guarantee Act (2005) guarantees up to 100 days of wage employment in a financial year to eligible rural households willing to do unskilled manual work.

Objectives

Employment Security
Women Empowerment

Sustainable Development

Key Features

  • 100 days guaranteed employment
  • Equal wages
  • Transparency & Social Audit
  • Job Cards
  • Gram Sabha participation
